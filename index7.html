Great Leaders of India

Mahatma Gandhi

Leader of India's independence movement, pioneer of non-violent resistance (Satyagraha).

Subhas Chandra Bose

Revolutionary leader, founder of the Indian National Army (INA), known as Netaji.

Bhagat Singh

Young revolutionary who fought against British rule, a symbol of courage and sacrifice.

Sardar Patel

Known as the Iron Man of India, he unified princely states into the Indian Union.

Jawaharlal Nehru

India’s first Prime Minister, architect of modern India, champion of democracy.

B. R. Ambedkar

Principal architect of the Indian Constitution, social reformer, Dalit leader.

Rani Lakshmibai

Queen of Jhansi, iconic freedom fighter in the Revolt of 1857.

Swami Vivekananda

Spiritual leader who introduced Indian philosophy to the West, inspired Indian youth.
